[03] 数学-简单
提问: 如果 3x + 5 = 20，x 等于多少？请给出步骤。
回答:
1.首先,我们 starts with the equation 3x + 5 = 20.
2.我们 want to solve for x, so we need to isolate x on one side of the equation.
3.我们 can start by subtracting 5 from both sides of the equation to get rid of the 5 on the left side. This gives us 3x = 20 - 5.
4. Simplifying the right side of the equation gives us 3x = 15.
5. Now, we need to divide both sides of the equation by 3 to isolate x. This gives us x = 15 / 3.
6. Simplifying the right side of the equation gives us x = 5.
因此, x equals 5.
